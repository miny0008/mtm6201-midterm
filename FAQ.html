FAQ

General Questions

What is the Food App?
The Food App is a mobile application that allows users to order food from various restaurants and have it delivered to their location.
How do I sign up?
To sign up, simply download the app from the App Store or Google Play Store and follow the registration instructions.

Ordering and Delivery

How do I place an order?
To place an order, open the app, select the restaurant and items you want to order, and proceed to checkout.
What are the delivery times?
Delivery times vary depending on the restaurant and your location. You can check estimated delivery times when placing your order.
